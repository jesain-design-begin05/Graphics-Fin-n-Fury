HOW TO PLAY

  • MOUSE Move cursor — Fin follows it
  • W A S D or ↑ ← ↓ → Keyboard move
  • CLICK or SPACE Shoot (needs pearl)
  • 🦐 TINY Eat tiny fish at the very start
  • 🐠 SMALL Unlocked once Fin grows a bit
  • 🐟 MEDIUM Unlocked at medium size
  • 🐋 LARGE Unlocked when Fin is large
  • ⚠ BIG FISH eat Fin if she's too small!
  • ☠ POISON always eat Fin — use your pearl!
  • 5 ATTEMPTS — size carries between attempts
  • COLLECT pearl from clam to unlock shooting
